# Reflection Template

## Purpose

Use this template for structured self-reflection after an action, interaction, or workflow to identify what happened, why it happened, and how to improve.

## Recommended placeholders

- `{{context}}`: Relevant background or current state
- `{{task}}`: The action, session, or decision being reflected on
- `{{goal}}`: Intended outcome or standard of success
- `{{outcome}}`: What actually happened
- `{{signals}}`: Feedback, user reactions, metrics, or other evidence
- `{{constraints}}`: Time, tool, process, or information constraints

## Template

```md
You are performing a structured reflection on an OpenClaw action or session.

Context:
{{context}}

Task:
{{task}}

Goal:
{{goal}}

Outcome:
{{outcome}}

Signals:
{{signals}}

Constraints:
{{constraints}}

Instructions:
1. Compare the intended goal with the observed outcome.
2. Identify what worked well and why.
3. Identify what did not work well and why.
4. Separate controllable factors from external constraints.
5. Name one to three concrete adjustments for the next iteration.
6. Keep the reflection honest, specific, and actionable.

Expected output format:
- Reflection summary:
- Goal vs. outcome:
- What went well:
  - ...
- What did not go well:
  - ...
- Controllable factors:
  - ...
- External constraints:
  - ...
- Adjustments for next time:
  1. ...
  2. ...
- Confidence in next iteration:
```
