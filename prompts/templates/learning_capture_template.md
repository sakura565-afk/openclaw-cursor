# Learning Capture Template

## Purpose

Use this template to capture durable learnings from a task, incident, or experiment so they can be reused later.

## Recommended placeholders

- `{{context}}`: Situation, project state, or scenario
- `{{task}}`: Activity that produced the learning
- `{{goal}}`: Intended result or decision target
- `{{outcome}}`: What happened in practice
- `{{evidence}}`: Logs, metrics, outputs, tests, or examples
- `{{applicability}}`: Where the learning is likely to transfer

## Template

```md
You are capturing durable learnings from an OpenClaw task or outcome.

Context:
{{context}}

Task:
{{task}}

Goal:
{{goal}}

Outcome:
{{outcome}}

Evidence:
{{evidence}}

Applicability:
{{applicability}}

Instructions:
1. Extract insights that are reusable, not just a retelling of events.
2. Distinguish between confirmed learnings and tentative hypotheses.
3. Explain why each learning matters and where it should be applied next.
4. Capture anti-patterns or pitfalls to avoid in future runs.
5. End with concrete updates to prompts, workflows, docs, or habits.

Expected output format:
- Situation:
- Confirmed learnings:
  1. Learning:
     - Evidence:
     - Why it matters:
     - Where to apply it:
- Tentative hypotheses:
  - ...
- Pitfalls to avoid:
  - ...
- Recommended updates:
  - Prompt updates:
  - Process updates:
  - Documentation updates:
- Follow-up experiments:
  - ...
```
